Our History

Rafting Compas was born from a passion to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with people of all ages and skill levels. Since our humble beginnings in 2015, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted and enthusiastic adventure outfitters.

.

Over the years, our team has guided thousands of adventurers through challenging rapids, breathtaking scenery, and unforgettable moments. Our commitment to safety, nature, and fun remains at the core of every trip we offer.
